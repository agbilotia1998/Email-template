About Us
Effervescence is the Cultural Festival of IIIT Allahabad (A Centre of Excellence in Information Technology established by Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India). Effervescence 2016 was a phenomenal success with footfalls exceeding 10,000, witnessing participation from over 75 colleges from all over India. This, with the fact that most of the horde at the fest was in the 17-26 age-group, is convincing proof of its acclaim among youth.

Main Events
Celebrity Night
Over the years, Effervescence has catapulted to the highest echelons of cultural brilliance, with previous years featuring performances by entertainment industry heavyweights such as Neha Kakkar, Sona Mohapatra, Daler Mehndi, Lucky Ali, Strings, Bombay Vikings, and many more. Effervescence has helped us become the fastest growing cultural festival in India.
